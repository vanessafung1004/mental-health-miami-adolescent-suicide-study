# Mental Health in Miami: Adolescent Suicide Study

## Overview
This project was completed as part of my graduate studies in Public Mental Health. It examines rising suicide risk among adolescents in Miami-Dade County and explores systemic barriers, cultural factors, and community-based prevention strategies.

## Topic
Increasing suicide rates among adolescents in Miami-Dade County, Florida.

## Focus Area
Hispanic and African American youth ages 13 to 18 in Miami-Dade County.

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Community mental health analysis
- Suicide risk assessment
- Culturally responsive intervention planning
- Research and evidence synthesis
- Public health writing
